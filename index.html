Our Heritage

Mughal Flavours

Founded in Helsinki, Mughal Indian Restaurant has been crafting the finest royal Indian cuisine for over 25 years. Every dish is inspired by the magnificent Mughal Empire — a tribute to history, spice and culture.

Our master chefs bring generations of culinary wisdom — from the smoky tandoors of the Punjab to the fragrant biryanis of Hyderabad — all under one royal roof in the heart of Helsinki.
